The ancient seal on the forgotten tomb has just crumbled under your touch, revealing a dark, descending stairway. A chilling wind escapes from within.
You find yourself accused of a crime you didn't commit in the bustling city of Silverhaven, with guards closing in.
A cryptic map, a gift from a dying hermit, leads you to the edge of the Whisperwood, rumored to hide an artifact of immense power.
You awaken in a strange glade, your memory hazy, with only a peculiar amulet around your neck and the sounds of a hunt nearby.
A desperate village elder pleads for your help against a monstrous beast that has been terrorizing their livestock.
